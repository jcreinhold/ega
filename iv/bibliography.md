# Bibliography (continued)

This continues the EGA bibliography across the four parts of EGA IV (1964, 1965, 1966, 1967). It inherits the numbering
from EGA II [1]–[26] and EGA III [27]–[29]; EGA IV adds entries [30]–[44]. Duplicates between parts are merged into a
single entry, with the parts in which each entry was cited recorded as a trailing note.
