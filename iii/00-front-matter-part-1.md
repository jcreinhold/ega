## Preliminaries

This part of the running Chapter 0 (preliminaries) gathers the categorical, algebraic, and homological material that EGA
III, and later volumes, will use. It picks up the numbering of Chapter 0 from EGA I at §8, and continues through §13.
§11 is reserved in the print; the numbering jumps from §10 directly to §12.

### Sommaire (Chapter 0, continued)

- **§8.** Representable functors.
- **§9.** Constructible sets.
- **§10.** Complements on flat modules.
- **§11.** Complements on homological algebra (spectral sequences, hypercohomology).
- **§12.** Complements on the cohomology of sheaves.
- **§13.** Projective limits in homological algebra.
