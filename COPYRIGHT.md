# Copyright and rights

This repository contains an English translation, in Markdown with Unicode mathematics, of Grothendieck and Dieudonné's
*Éléments de Géométrie Algébrique* (EGA).

Two distinct layers of rights apply.

## Underlying French text

The original *Éléments de Géométrie Algébrique* was published in *Publications mathématiques de l'IHÉS*, tomes 4, 8,
11, 17, 20, 24, 28, and 32 (1960–1967), with A. Grothendieck and J. Dieudonné as authors. The original text is
© the original authors and publishers (IHÉS), all rights reserved.

This translation is offered as a non-commercial scholarly aid, in the spirit of Grothendieck's known wishes for the
free circulation of mathematics, and in line with the *fair use* / *usage loyal* provisions for scholarship and
education. No grant of rights in the underlying French text is made or implied by this repository.

The French source text was drawn from the Grothendieck Circle's archive of Grothendieck's published writings:
<https://webusers.imj-prg.fr/~leila.schneps/grothendieckcircle/pubtexts.php>.
