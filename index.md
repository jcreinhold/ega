# Éléments de Géométrie Algébrique — English

An idiomatic, LLM-generated English translation (Markdown, LaTeX mathematics)
of Grothendieck and Dieudonné's *Éléments de Géométrie Algébrique*
(*Publications mathématiques de l'IHÉS*, 1960–1967).

## Volumes

- [Volume I — The Language of Schemes](i/00-front-matter.md)
- [Volume II — Some Classes of Morphisms](ii/00-front-matter.md)
- [Volume III — Cohomology of Coherent Sheaves](iii/00-front-matter-part-1.md)
- [Volume IV — Local Study of Schemes and Morphisms](iv/00-front-matter-part-1.md)
- [Volume V — Construction of Schemes (unpublished)](v/00-front-matter.md)

## Translation principles

- Preserve EGA's historical terminology (notably *prescheme* / *scheme*); inline the
  modern gloss on first occurrence per section, and keep a per-volume
  translation glossary.
- Preserve EGA's decimal numbering exactly: $(N.M.K)$ within a chapter,
  $(0, N.M.K)$ for Chapter 0 references, $(I, N.M.K)$ for cross-chapter references.
- Mark original page transitions inline as HTML comments
  (`<!-- original page n -->`).
- Revise prose for English mathematical idiom while preserving Grothendieck's
  architectural voice.
